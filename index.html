  • Hydrothermal Vents

    You are perhaps grown up with the knowledge that sunlight is the source of all energy produced in earth’s biosphere. At least I had that idea – until I came to know of ‘hydrothermal vents’ and life around them. Thousands of cracks are there in the magma chambers beneath the ocean bed. They create a chain of hydrothermal vents all over the ocean floor. These vents spew extremely hot waters which are rich in minerals - coming out of the earth’s inner layers – and also provide life’s ingredients. Water around these vents has temperature way above the normal boiling point – but they don’t boil because of heavy pressure. It might be unimaginable to see life in those waters! But behold – there exists a complete biological world there without any link to the sun. Instead of photosynthesis, food in this world is generated through chemical processes without sunlight – termed as ‘chemosynthesis’. To know more on this process click here. And watch this short introduction to this ‘sunless’ world of life – 6 minutes
